What we do

Mabool, it’s a glimpse in tomorrow’s mobility

Mabool aims to be your unique partner for your fleet of shared mobility. Bike, solowheel, scooter … we propose many complementary solutions for tomorrow’s mobility.

Rebalancing, cleaning, charging, fueling… Mabool offers a whole range of services, quick and clean. CO² neutral. Guaranteed.

Values

Mission

Mabool wants to be a central player in tomorrow’s cities.

For us, era of ownership has come to an end : sharing economy is a revolution for mobility. Mabool plays there, one step forward : fast and green !

Public space is a common good. Giving it back as much as possible to its real owners (citizen) is our value statement and a concrete goal.

Cities for people and smart mobility are dream and reality of Mabool’s work !

Services

Therefore Mabool provides a whole range of 0 emission services specially designed for shared mobility :


Rebalancing 

We are specialists of rebalancing shared fleet following 3 principles : fast, silent and clean.

Whatever your need is, we propose the adapted service for shared fleet. Folding, electric bike, trot, solo wheel… we’ll choose the most efficient tool to fit your needs and get rid of the city’s numerous traps.

Charging

We offer flexibility and efficiency for electric charging as for classical fueling.

Cleaning

We have special trained workers to take care of inter and extern cleaning of your fleet, with the smallest environmental impact (dry and ecological cleaning).

Emergency services

Our speciality ! Emergency repark, towing… the flexibility and rapidity of bike in one goal : react quickly to avoid bigger problems.

Mabool also offers a range of other services needed by a fleet manager, by taking care of:

maintenance, technical check… or any tailored made offer.
